Recipe for Potato Soup

  1. Bring chicken broth to a simmer.
  2. Chop potatoes into desired sizes (I do quarters) and add to the broth.
  3. Chop onion half into chunks and add to broth.
  4. Cook bacon.
  5. After bacon has been cooked and placed to the side, slowly add flour to the bacon grease.
  6. Add milk to the oil and flour mixture until it is a milky paste.
  7. Add the milky paste into the broth and stir until it is uniform.
  8. Add milk and stir until the soup becomes desired consistency.

Now we have a creamy loaded potato soup. Enjoy!
